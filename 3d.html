Futuristic Taxi

I modeled this taxi based around a cyberpunk aesthetic for one of my classes. I used textured and Arnold surfaces to help create detail for the taxi. By clicking on the pictures you can see a short animation of the taxi moving in a simple Maya environment.



Last Day on Earth

I modeled this room with inspiration from the Netflix movie I am Mother. I set this really modern room on desolate planet Earth. The story being that this room runs also as a space ship and is set to take off to Earth 2 once it is ready. By clicking on the pictures you can see the partial animation for the countdown to take off.
