Mona (Chillenium Game Jam 2022)

Though I have not worked on many games this is my first game completed. MONA was made during a 48 hour game jam in April 2022 and in its simplicity, You help Mona Lisa return back to the Lourve in a Cyperpunk themed world fitting for the game jam topic of "You can not mix those". I worked on most of the coding in Gamemaker and also designed the levels. I hope to potentially pursue a career in level design and game development.
